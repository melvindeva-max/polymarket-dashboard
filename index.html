Position Sizing Guide

Kelly Criterion Lite

For each bet, allocate based on your edge:

ConfidenceMax AllocationRisk LevelExample
90%+20-25% of bankrollLow (1)Near-certain outcomes like confirmed nominations
70-89%15-20% of bankrollMedium (2)Strong favorites with some uncertainty
50-69%8-12% of bankrollMedium (2)Coin-flip markets where you have an edge
Below 50%5-8% of bankrollHigh (3)Speculative longshots with asymmetric payoff

Risk Management Rules

1. Diversify across categories. Never put more than 40% of bankroll in one category (sports, politics, crypto, etc.).

2. Time diversification. Mix short-term (<1 month), medium-term (1-3 months), and long-term (3-6 months) bets.

3. Always keep 15-20% cash. New opportunities arise constantly ‚Äî dry powder lets you act fast.

4. Cut losers early. If a position moves against you by 30%+ and the thesis is broken, sell rather than hoping.

5. Let winners run. If a position is trending toward resolution in your favor, hold for max payout rather than selling early at a discount.
