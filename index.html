EV Calculator

Expected Value = (Prob of Win × Payout if Win) − (Prob of Loss × Cost if Loss)

Example: Buy YES at 40¢, you believe true probability is 55%.
EV = (0.55 × $0.60) − (0.45 × $0.40) = $0.33 − $0.18 = +$0.15 per share

Only take bets with EV > +$0.05 per share. Below that, fees and slippage erode the edge.

Risk Management

  • Time Decay: Markets near expiry with low probability become lottery tickets. Size accordingly.
