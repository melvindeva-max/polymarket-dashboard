Strategy Guide

Position Sizing Rules

Kelly Criterion (simplified): Bet size = (edge / odds). Never bet more than 20% of bankroll on a single position.

Risk tiers:
• Risk 1 (High confidence, 85%+): Up to 20% of available cash
• Risk 2 (Medium, 60-84%): Up to 15% of available cash
• Risk 3 (Speculative, <60%): Max 10% of available cash

Portfolio mix target: ~45% high-confidence, ~40% medium, ~15% speculative

Expected Value (EV) Calculator

Formula: EV = (prob_win × payout) - (prob_lose × stake)

Example: BUY YES @ 40¢, you believe true prob = 55%
EV = (0.55 × $0.60) - (0.45 × $0.40) = $0.33 - $0.18 = +$0.15 per share

Rules of thumb:
• Only trade when EV > 5¢ per share
• Higher EV markets deserve larger allocations
• Factor in time: longer duration = higher opportunity cost
