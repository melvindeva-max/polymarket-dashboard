Risk Management Tips

1.Never bet more than 20% of bankroll on a single position. Even "sure things" can lose.
2.Diversify across categories. Correlated bets amplify drawdowns.
3.Check liquidity before entering. Markets under $5K liquidity have high slippage.
4.Set exit rules. If your thesis breaks, sell immediately ‚Äî don't hope.
5.Track every trade. Review P/L weekly. Cut losers, ride winners.
