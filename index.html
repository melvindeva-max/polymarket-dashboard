Position Sizing

Kelly Criterion (Simplified)

Optimal bet size = (bp - q) / b, where b = odds, p = win probability, q = 1 - p. For prediction markets, never bet more than half-Kelly to account for estimation error.

  • High confidence (85%+): Allocate 15-25% of bankroll per position
  • Medium confidence (60-84%): Allocate 10-20% per position
  • Speculative (<60%): Cap at 10-15% per position

Risk Management Rules
