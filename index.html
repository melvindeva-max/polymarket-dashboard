üìê Position Sizing Framework

Use the Kelly Criterion for optimal sizing. Never risk more than 5% of bankroll on a single trade.

  • High Confidence (85%+): 5-15% of bankroll. These are your bread and butter.
  • Medium Confidence (60-84%): 3-8% of bankroll. Core positions with reasonable edge.
  • Speculative (<60%): 1-3% of bankroll. Lottery tickets with asymmetric upside.
